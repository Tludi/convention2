Wildwood Gardens

Tracy and Will Plotner’s garden is located southeast of Molalla, Oregon along the wild Molalla River. The over 400 official guest irises will be found in the upper garden near the road.

Visitors will also find some recent introductions and seedlings of Lee Walker and Will Plotner
