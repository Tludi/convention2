Schreiner's Iris Garden

Located along I-5 just north of Salem, Oregon, the Schreiner’s Iris Gardens has long been associated with the hybridizing and growing of quality Tall Bearded and Median Irises.

The large 10 acre display garden contains a master planting of 100 Beardless official guest irises and over 400 Bearded official guest irises.

Few gardens surpass the 10 acre display in a 50 year old garden. The iris is grown with an array of mature perennials.
