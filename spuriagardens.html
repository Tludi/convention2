Spuria / Species Garden Tours

Convention attendees will revisit the Aitken’s Salmon Creek Garden, Mt Pleasant Iris Farm, Mid-America Gardens and Schreiner’s Iris Gardens. In addition, attendees will visit Kevin Vaughn’s garden located just south of Salem, Oregon. As the saying goes “IF SOMETHING HAS POLLEN, KEVIN WILL PLAY WITH IT”.

Located just across the Columbia River in Vancouver, Washington. Barbara and Terry Aitken’s garden contains a master planting of more than 950 Bearded and Beardless official guest irises.
