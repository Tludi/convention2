Mid-America Gardens

Located northeast of Salem, OR, this garden contains a master planting of more than 800 Bearded official guest irises. This garden is home to the hybridizing efforts of Thomas Johnson and Paul Black. Visitors should take time to wonder around the house to view the large landscaped display of unusual trees, shrubs and exotic plants. In addition, the large collection of hostas and shade plants of Sebright Gardens awaits your viewing pleasure.
