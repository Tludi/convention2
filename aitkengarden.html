Aitken's Salmon Creek Garden

Aitken's Salmon Creek Garden

Located just across the Columbia River in Vancouver, Washington. Barbara and Terry Aitken’s garden contains a master planting of more than 950 Bearded and Beardless official guest irises. The Beardless, Medians and Tall Bearded guest irises are planted in separate areas of the garden. Visitors will have a real treat to view the introductions of hybridizers Terry Aitken, Chuck Bunnell, Ken Fisher, Chad Harris, Lynn Markham, Stephanie Markham, Lynda Miller, Richard Richards, Marky Smith, Terry Varner, Kevin Vaughn and Jean Witt. The northwest slope is devoted to irises grown by Cynthia Bauer and Bruce Filardi, including Bruce’s large historic iris collection. One should also take time to view the orchid collection in the large greenhouse.
